What is Circa?
Circa is a service for the Georgia Tech community that allows students to buy and sell electronics with each other. We handle all the logistics including pricing, payment, shipping, and fraud protection. No more haggling over prices with multiple people, coordinating awkward meetups, giving contact information to strangers, or getting scammed!

How does buying on Circa work?
  1. Bid on an item (or use the Buy Now option to skip to step 3)
  2. Win the auction
  3. Pay securely online
  4. The item arrives at your doorstep the next day
  5. Profit???

How does selling on Circa work?
  1. Post your item, and share the link with others
  2. Tell us when to pick it up.
  3. We pick up the item and you get paid. Circa takes a 10% commission to help us cover payment processing, delivery costs, and hosting expenses.
  4. Profit!!!

How do returns on Circa work?
  1. Tell us you want to return the item within 30 days of purchase. No questions asked.
  2. Tell us when we should pick it up.
  3. We pick it up, you get your money back!

Is my credit card number safe on Circa?
Our site uses Secure Sockets Layer to force HTTPS on every page, ensuring that data transferred between your computer and Circa is encrypted. Circa uses Stripe for payment processing, so your credit card number never touches our server. Even if we're hacked, your credit card is safe. You can learn more about Stripe's security measures here.
If you find a security issue or bug of any other kind, please email support@usecirca.com immediately and we'll resolve it as soon as possible.

Where is Circa available?
Circa is available to all the zip codes around Georgia Tech: 30308, 30309, 30313, 30318, and 30332. Shoot us an email (support@usecirca.com) if you want us to expand to your zip code!

I still need help! What do I do?
Email us at support@usecirca.com. We'll get back to you in under 24 hours or we'll buy you donuts!
