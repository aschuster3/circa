About

Circa is a Georgia Tech startup that removes the hassle of buying and selling with your neighbors. We handle money, delivery, and returns so you don't have to!

No Haggling

Sellers list their items and set an asking price. Buyers make offers. If an offer remains undefeated for an hour, it is automatically accepted.

Fast Delivery

We pick up the item from the seller and deliver to the buyer within 24 hours. The seller is paid electronically. Goodbye, awkward meetups!

Easy Returns

Unhappy with your purchase? Let us know within 30 days of purchase and you get a full refund, no questions asked. Never get scammed again!

How It Works

Selling

  • Post your item
  • Item gets sold
  • We pick up the item
  • You get paid*
*Circa takes a commission of $2 or 15% (whichever is greater) for delivery costs.

Buying

  • Make an offer
  • Offer accepted in an hour*
  • Pay securely online
  • Item arrives on your doorstep
*As long as no higher offers are made in that time.
