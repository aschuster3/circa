About

Circa is a Georgia Tech startup that removes the hassle of buying and selling with your neighbors. We handle money, delivery, and returns so you don't have to!

No Haggling

Sellers list their items and set an asking price. Buyers make offers. If an offer remains undefeated for an hour, it is automatically accepted.

Fast Delivery

We pick up the item from the seller and deliver to the buyer within 24 hours. The seller is paid electronically. Awkward meetups are a thing of the past!

Easy returns

Unhappy with your purchase? Let us know within 30 days of purchase and you get a full refund, no questions asked. Never get scammed again!

How It Works

Buying

Buying on Circa is easy!

  1. Make an offer on an item (or use the Buy Now option to skip to step 3)
  2. If nobody beats your offer in an hour, your offer is automatically accepted!
  3. Pay securely online
  4. The item arrives at your doorstep the next day
  5. Profit???

Selling

Sell your stuff and make money with just a few clicks!

  1. Post your item, create a starting offer and buy now price, and share the link with others
  2. We pick up the item and you get paid. Circa takes a commission of $2 or 15% (whichever is greater) to help us cover payment processing, delivery costs, and hosting expenses.
  3. Profit!!!
