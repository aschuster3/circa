About

Circa is a Georgia Tech startup that takes the hassle out of buying and selling with your neighbors. We handle payments, delivery, and returns so you don't have to!

Easy Buying

Sellers list their items, and buyers purchase them with two clicks. No hassles, no worries.

Easy Delivery

We'll take it from here. We pick up and deliver the item within 24 hours, and all payments are handled online. Goodbye, awkward meetups!

Easy Returns

Unhappy with your purchase? Let us know within 30 days and you'll get a full refund, no questions asked. No tricks, no scams, no tears - just love.

How It Works

The local buying process made simple

Selling

  • Post your item
  • Item gets sold
  • We pick up the item
  • You get paid through Venmo, PayPal, or direct deposit*

  • Pay securely online

  • Item arrives on your doorstep
