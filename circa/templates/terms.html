Buying

Shipping

You will receive your item within 24 hours of us picking it up from the seller. Since the seller determines when we pick up the item, this may result in you receiving your item more than 24 hours after ordering. Most of the time, you'll receive it the same or next day.

Returns

You may return your item within 30 days of purchase and receive a full refund. To do so, simply email support@usecirca.com requesting a return and telling us when you'd like us to pick it up. Upon picking up the item, we will immediately refund your credit card for the full amount paid for the item. Depending on how long it takes your bank to process the refund, it may take 5-10 days before it appears on your statement.


In rare cases, we may decline a refund request if it's clear you significantly damaged or altered the item while it was in your possession. As of this writing, we've never declined a refund request.
